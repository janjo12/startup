    This game tests internal rhythm by requiring the player to keep a counter going in their head.

    The timer will count up to 5 seconds, then continue on invisibly. Meanwhile, the player must mark exactly when the invisible timer reaches the 10, 15, 20, 25, and 30 second marks. The final score is made by adding up the 5 differences between the marked time and the actual time on the invisible timer.
